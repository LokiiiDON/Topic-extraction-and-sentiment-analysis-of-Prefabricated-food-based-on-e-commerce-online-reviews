Prepared meal reviews were analyzed using 6,000 comments. LDA and SnowNLP were applied after cleaning. Results show logistics, taste, price, and cooking conditions are key factors. Most consumers are positive, but some feedback shows issues. Suggestions are proposed to improve quality and satisfaction.
I led a data-driven project to understand product pain points by analyzing low-rated customer reviews. The key challenge: how to systematically identify what users actually complain about among thousands of reviews? 

My approach: 

Designed a data collection strategy: monitored JD.com API to capture 980 low-rated reviews (score ≤4) 

Analyzed the data using LDA topic modeling to automatically identify recurring pain points 

Translated findings into actionable product recommendations prioritized by impact
